Publishing workflow

One data file, repeatable news updates.

The news page is designed so the team can regularly add articles without rebuilding the visual layout. Add a new item to the news data file, upload any supporting image, and deploy.

  • Write a short hunter-facing brief, not a generic press release.
  • Attach the source link for event dates or industry claims.
  • Use field-relevant categories: trail cameras, bowhunting, outdoor technology, conservation, and event watch.
  • Keep every article tied to a practical question: what should a hunter watch, learn, buy, prepare, or ask the agent to analyze?
Content standard

Each update should translate news into a hunting workflow: camera setup, target patterning, route planning, weather timing, gear readiness, or post-hunt learning.
