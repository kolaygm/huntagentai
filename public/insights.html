What the agent analyzes

A hunting decision is a data problem with field consequences.

HuntAgent AI keeps the hunter in control, but gives the hunter a stronger read: what moved, where it moved, why it may move again, and how to approach without ruining the setup.

  • Trail-camera captures, timestamps, target matches, and empty trigger history.
  • Wind, temperature, pressure, moon, sunrise, sunset, and legal shooting-light reminders.
  • Map layers, creek lines, timber edges, access routes, and likely movement funnels.
  • Your corrections and post-hunt notes, so the agent gets better with every outing.
